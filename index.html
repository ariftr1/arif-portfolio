Building a system, not just an interface.

I am Arif, a final-year student pursuing a Diploma IV in Informatics Engineering at Universitas Harkat Negeri. I have a strong interest in software development, particularly in building applications that integrate frontend, backend, database, and AI-based technologies.

I have experience developing applications using Flutter, Laravel, Java, Python, and MySQL/PostgreSQL. One of the projects I am currently developing is **MyoGuard**, an eye health monitoring application built with Flutter (using the GetX architecture), a Flask backend, a Neon PostgreSQL database, and features such as JWT authentication, email OTP, Google Sign-In, and risk prediction using the Random Forest algorithm. Additionally, I am developing a management information system for a rice milling business as part of my final project.

Beyond software development, I have experience in computer assembly and troubleshooting, and I am proficient in working with Linux. I enjoy learning new technologies, solving problems systematically, and building solutions that provide value to users.
